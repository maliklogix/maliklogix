## 🧠 Who We Are

</div>

**[MalikLogix](https://maliklogix.com)** is a **Pakistan-based Digital Marketing & AI Automation agency** founded in **2020** by **Malik Farooq** (BSc Computer Science, University of Sargodha). We help e-commerce brands and growth-stage businesses scale faster using a combination of **AI-powered marketing**, **intelligent automation**, and **production-grade AI systems**.

We don't do generic campaigns or cookie-cutter automation. Every engagement is custom-built — from **AI-driven lead funnels** and **SEO/GEO content engines** to **multi-agent LLM pipelines** and **end-to-end n8n workflows** — designed to deliver measurable ROI, not just vanity metrics.

> 💬 *"We don't just run campaigns. We engineer growth — with AI at the core."*
> — **Malik Farooq, Founder & CEO, [MalikLogix](https://maliklogix.com)**
